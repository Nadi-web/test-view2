Our primary genre can be characterized as progressive metalcore, but we also incorporate elements from various rock and metal genres such as math-metal , djent, post-metal, black metal, and others. We use both clean and extreme vocals in English.We produce different metal that is equally heavy and melodic, combining the full musical range of the genre to enrich and diversify our sound.
